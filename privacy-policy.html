PRIVACY

Protecting your privacy and handling your information.

At Maid At Home, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. We take all necessary measures to protect your personal information.

Privacy Policy

At Maid At Home, we take your privacy seriously. We have developed this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read the following details carefully.

  • We will clearly identify the purpose for collecting personal information before or at the time of collection.
  • The personal information we collect will only be used for the purposes specified and compatible purposes, unless we have your consent or are required by law.
  • We will retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill those purposes.
  • We will collect personal information in a lawful and fair manner, and where appropriate, with the individual’s knowledge or consent.
  • The personal data we collect will be relevant to its intended use and will be kept accurate, complete, and up-to-date to the best of our ability.
  • We will implement reasonable security measures to protect personal information from loss, theft, and unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.
  • We are committed to making information about our privacy practices and policies readily available to customers.
  • We are dedicated to ensuring that personal information is handled in a way that maintains confidentiality and is protected according to these principles.
