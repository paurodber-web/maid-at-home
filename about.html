Our Story

More than a cleaning company, we are your allies in well-being.

Maid At Home was born from a shared frustration: the lack of professionalism and "eye" for detail in traditional services. We saw how trust was fragmented between late arrivals and mediocre results.

We decided to break the mold. We didn't want to be another mass outsourcing platform, but a boutique team where integrity and detail were not extras, but the minimum standard.

Today, we differentiate ourselves by our obsessive selection and our ability to understand that we don't clean houses, but care for sanctuaries. It's our way of giving respect back to every space.
